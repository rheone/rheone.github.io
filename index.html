Robert H. Engelhardt

I've been a professional software developer for over sixteen years aiding the National Nuclear Security Administration (NNSA) and the Department of Energy (DOE) in mission critical activities.

My most recent decade-long tenure has been at Sandia National Laboratories (SNL) as a Principal R&D S&E Computer Scientist and Team Lead

I have previously worked for Honeywell Federal Manufacturing & Technologies in Albuquerque now associated with Kansas City National Security Campus (KCNSC).

I reside in New Mexico where I keep the company of a couple of cats, and small flock of retired chickens on about an acre of land where I keep claiming I'll at some point make good use of.
